2048: How to

2048 is a small puzzle game that ended up taking the world by storm in a very unexpected way. In this game, you move tiles on a 4x4 Grid. When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge and their value doubles. To win, you need to reach the coveted “2048” tile.


Use your arrow keys to move the tiles.
